About Lottery Simulator

Lottery Simulator is an educational game that demonstrates the real odds of winning lottery games. Instead of spending your hard-earned money on actual lottery tickets, you can experience the same excitement virtually and learn about probability in the process.

This simulator was created to help people understand just how unlikely it is to win big lottery prizes, while still enjoying the fun of playing.
